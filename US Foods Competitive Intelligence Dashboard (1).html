üî¥ Universal Across All Distributors

  • Driver Shortage / Late Deliveries: Chronic capacity constraint; Technomic reports 4-6% of expected deliveries slip.
  • Labor Cost Volatility: 37% youth labor participation declining; wage pressure intensifying.
  • Order Minimums & Flexibility: Distributors demand rigid minimum order sizes; operators want flexibility.
  • Digital Ordering Friction: 4% satisfaction drop online vs. in-person (Technomic). Operators report clunky checkouts.
  • Food Cost Transparency: Operators struggle to track true per-item costs vs. promotional vs. list prices.
  • Menu Price Increases Without Notice: Operators report ingredient substitutions and price increases mid-week.

üî¥ Sysco-Specific Frustrations

  • Perception of Frozen Food: Viral anti-Sysco sentiment: "same frozen food in every restaurant."
  • National Account Pressure: Sysco prioritizes national chains; independents get deprioritized.
  • Digital Experience Lag: Pricing agility is new (Q3 2025); before that, reps couldn't match competitor pricing.
  • Product Damage in Delivery: "Crushed eggs below stacks of boxes" is a recurring complaint.

üî¥ US Foods-Specific Frustrations

  • Product Breadth Gaps: Smaller portfolio vs. Sysco. Operators report "US Foods doesn't have X, have to use Sysco."
  • Regional Coverage Inconsistency: West Coast & South presence weaker; delivery times longer in growth regions.
  • MOXƒì Learning Curve: While praised, app has onboarding friction for older operators.

üî¥ PFG-Specific Frustrations

  • Digital Experience Fragmentation: Separate portals for foodservice vs. convenience vs. Vistar.
  • Integration Indigestion: Core-Mark & Eby-Brown acquisitions still have legacy system silos.
  • Visibility Gap: PFG lacks the thought leadership & industry presence of Sysco/US Foods.
